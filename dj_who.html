D.J. Who?

D.J. Moore aka "Mr.Third Down" has had an impeccable season so far for the Carolina Panthers. Denniston Moore Jr. or D.J. back when he was at the University of Maryland football team. He was more electric. His freshmen year his stats were 25 receptions for 357 yards & 1 rushing attempt for 9 yards combining for 366 yards in total theat season. His sophomore season was even better. Coming up with 41 receptions for 637 yards & 11 rushes for 55 yards combining for 692 yards. D.J.'s last year was the greatest in Maryland football history. 80 receptions for 1,033 & 61 rushes for 85, combining for 1,094 yards. Adding up his whole career stats at Unversity of Maryland. he came up with 2,027 receiving yards, 125 rushing yards that combines for 2,152 yards total. D.J. Moore was drafted the 24 pick overall in the 2018 NFL draft.
